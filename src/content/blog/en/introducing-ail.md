Axomiya IT Labs is an open-source initiative built for Assam, by Assamese developers and enthusiasts. Our mission is simple: make artificial intelligence, software development, and open-source contribution accessible to everyone in the region, regardless of their English proficiency or technical background.

## Why we started

Technology has always been a barrier for many people in Assam. Not because they lack intelligence or curiosity, but because most tools, tutorials, and documentation are in English. If you do not speak English fluently, you are already at a disadvantage before you even open a laptop. We decided to change that.

We believe the best technology is not the most complicated — it is the most inclusive. That is why every tutorial, tool, and project we publish comes in both Assamese and English. Language should never be a barrier to learning, building, or contributing.

## What we are building

Right now, we are working on several projects:

- **Assamese AI Chatbot** — An AI-powered chatbot that understands Assamese and helps students, professionals, and curious minds find answers in their own language.
- **Git & GitHub Tutorials** — A series of beginner-friendly guides that teach version control and open-source contribution in simple Assamese and English.
- **Translation Platform** — A community-driven platform to translate open-source documentation into Assamese, making global knowledge accessible locally.

We are also hosting online workshops and building local study groups so learners can meet, code, and grow together.

## Our values

Everything we do follows three principles: openness, accessibility, and community. Open source means our code is free for anyone to use, study, modify, and share. Accessibility means we build in Assamese first, then translate to English and other languages. Community means we do not build alone — we build with students, teachers, developers, and anyone who wants to contribute.

## How you can join

You do not need to be a programmer. You can contribute by writing content, translating, testing projects, reporting bugs, or simply sharing our work with others. If you have ever felt that tech was not made for people like you, we are here to prove that wrong. Welcome to Axomiya IT Labs — learn, build, and share with us.
